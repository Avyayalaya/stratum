# Domain Overview: Cognitive Mastery

## What is Cognitive Mastery?
**Cognitive Mastery** is the ability to think clearly, decide effectively, and learn rapidly — especially under pressure. It is the mental core of high-performance work. When hiring, Cognitive Mastery signals how well someone will navigate ambiguous problems without freezing or flailing.

This domain distinguishes elite strategists, resilient founders, and product thinkers. It doesn't just enable smart thinking — it enables **forward movement when the path isn't clear**.

> "In foggy terrain with no map, Cognitive Mastery becomes the GPS you build as you walk."

---

## Why This Domain Matters

Cognitive Mastery enables:
- Clear thinking when faced with complexity
- Action without needing perfect information
- Iteration based on learning — not ego
- Pattern recognition across contexts

It underpins high-leverage work in startups, strategy, AI/ML, product management, and crisis navigation. Teams that hire for Cognitive Mastery build cultures where ambiguity is an asset, not a blocker.

## The 5 Meta-Skills

| # | Meta-Skill | What It Enables |
|---|------------|-----------------|
| 1 | [First Principles Thinking](./first-principles-thinking.md) | Break down problems to fundamentals before solving |
| 2 | [Decision-Making Under Uncertainty](./decision-making.md) | Act decisively without full information |
| 3 | [Scenario Thinking](./scenario-thinking.md) | Simulate multiple futures before committing |
| 4 | [Feedback Calibration](./feedback-calibration.md) | Integrate signal without distortion or overreaction |
| 5 | [Bayesian Updating](./bayesian-updating.md) | Revise beliefs proportionally to new evidence |

---

## How These Skills Reinforce Each Other

The five skills form a flywheel. First Principles Thinking clarifies the problem space. Decision-Making Under Uncertainty moves you forward. Scenario Thinking maps the landscape of consequences. Feedback Calibration brings in real-world signal. Bayesian Updating refines your model — and the loop restarts with sharper fundamentals.

See the full flywheel case study: [Cognitive Mastery Flywheel](../framework/cognitive-mastery-flywheel.md)

## Coaching and Evaluation

- **Simulations**: Run ambiguous scenarios with structured debriefs
- **Socratic Drills**: Break down a known decision from first principles
- **Feedback Logs**: Track version histories of key decisions to surface calibration skill
- **Learning Journals**: Reflect on how something new was learned fast
