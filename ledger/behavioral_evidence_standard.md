# Ledger: Behavioral Evidence Standard

> **Status: audited (v1.0) — 2026-07-06.** Supporting evidence and counter-evidence triggers populated in the Phase 1.1 architecture audit; every citation DOI-verified via CrossRef. Counter-evidence is not a disclaimer — it operationalizes the framework's stance that literature is a feeder, not a ratifier. The triggers state the conditions under which this decision gets revised.

## Decision

Stratum scores behavioral evidence — specific Situation + Action + Outcome stories — over trait self-report. Trait claims ("I'm decisive," "I'm resilient") without behavioral evidence score 2 (Trait Claim) on the 1–5 rubric, regardless of subjective conviction. The standard rejects self-report as primary input because Dunning-Kruger demonstrates that self-assessment is structurally blind to development need: the people who most need to develop a meta-skill are the ones most likely to rate themselves highest on it. Stratum's evidence quality gate requires observable behavior in a specific past situation, scored by someone other than the subject.

## Counter-Evidence Triggers

| Trigger | Threshold | Action |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| The Dunning-Kruger pattern is substantially a statistical artifact (regression to the mean + better-than-average bias), not purely a metacognitive deficit (Krueger & Mueller 2002, [10.1037/0022-3514.82.2.180](https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-3514.82.2.180); Gignac & Zajenkowski 2020, [10.1016/j.intell.2020.101449](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.intell.2020.101449)). | Any reliance on Dunning-Kruger as the *sole* justification for rejecting self-report. | Drop D-K as the load-bearing argument. Rest the standard on the stronger, less-contested observer-accuracy evidence (Connelly & Ones, Vazire, Oh et al.). |
| When measurement noise is controlled, self-assessments do carry valid information about competence (Nuhfer et al. 2016, [10.5038/1936-4660.9.1.4](https://doi.org/10.5038/1936-4660.9.1.4); 2017, [10.5038/1936-4660.10.1.4](https://doi.org/10.5038/1936-4660.10.1.4)). | Treating self-report as categorically worthless. | Use self-assessment as a supplementary signal — the self–observer gap is itself a calibration diagnostic — not as a banned input. |
