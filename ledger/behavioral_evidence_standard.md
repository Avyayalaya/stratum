# Ledger: Behavioral Evidence Standard

> **Status: scaffold (v0.0).** This entry will be populated during Phase 1.1 architecture audit. The Decision below reflects pre-audit state per STR-001 thesis v1.0.

## Decision

Stratum scores behavioral evidence — specific Situation + Action + Outcome stories — over trait self-report. Trait claims ("I'm decisive," "I'm resilient") without behavioral evidence score 2 (Trait Claim) on the 1–5 rubric, regardless of subjective conviction. The standard rejects self-report as primary input because Dunning-Kruger demonstrates that self-assessment is structurally blind to development need: the people who most need to develop a meta-skill are the ones most likely to rate themselves highest on it. Stratum's evidence quality gate requires observable behavior in a specific past situation, scored by someone other than the subject.
